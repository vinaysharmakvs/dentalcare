FAQs

Questions patients ask before booking.

Is root canal treatment painful?

Modern root canal treatment is performed with local anesthesia and comfort-focused techniques, so most patients feel only mild pressure.

How long does teeth whitening take?

Most whitening appointments take 45 to 60 minutes, depending on the shade and sensitivity plan.

Do you treat children?

Yes. Pediatric dentistry appointments are designed to be calm, friendly and confidence-building for children.

Can I book an emergency appointment?

Yes. Call the emergency number or use WhatsApp for urgent tooth pain, swelling, broken teeth or dental injury.
